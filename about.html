About Us

Home / About

Making Exam Days Stress-Free

Exam Centre Helper was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to reduce the stress and anxiety that students experience on exam days. We understand that finding your exam center, locating amenities, and managing time effectively can be challenging.

Our platform connects students with essential services around exam centers, including cafes, printing shops, transportation options, and accommodation. We've helped over 50,000 students across India have a smoother exam experience.

Our team of dedicated professionals works tirelessly to ensure that our platform remains updated with accurate information and continues to meet the evolving needs of students.
